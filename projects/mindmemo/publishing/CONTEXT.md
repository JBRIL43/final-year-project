# Publishing Workspace

> **New agent:** Read [`../CONTEXT.md`](../CONTEXT.md) and [`../STATUS.md`](../STATUS.md) before this file.

## Purpose

Everything required for a successful v1 App Store launch: submission checklist, privacy nutrition labels, store listing copy, keyword notes, screenshot creative brief, and final pre-submit review.

## Process

1. **Submission checklist** — Maintain `checklist/app-store.md` (capabilities, entitlements, export compliance, on-device AI disclosure).
2. **Privacy** — Document data collection (none off-device) for App Privacy questionnaire; align with PRD privacy claims.
3. **Store listing** — `store-assets/listing.md`: title, subtitle, description, keywords research notes.
4. **Creative brief** — `store-assets/screenshots-brief.md`: screens, captions, device frames (iOS first).
5. **Build verification** — Release build, TestFlight notes, crash-free smoke test on device.
6. **Final review** — Walk checklist end-to-end; sign-off recorded in checklist file.
7. Update `../STATUS.md` to **Done** when App Store reports approved.

## What good output looks like

- Checklist is binary (done/not done)—no vague “mostly ready.”
- Listing copy matches actual MVP features (no promised cloud sync unless shipped).
- Screenshot brief is actionable by you or a designer without reading the codebase.
- Privacy answers match on-device-only architecture.
